BOOKINGS AND REFUND

 Can I refund my ticket(s)?

Yes, you can request for refunding of tickets 2 hours prior to the start of the show. Online refunding is unavailable. Offer tickets are non-refundable.




 What forms of payment do you accept for online bookings?

We accept credit and debit card and as well as google pay for booking the tickets. On counter tickets, as of now, are unavailable.
